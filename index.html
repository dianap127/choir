Hello!

We are a friendly local choir with around 25 singers and welcome new members with or without previous choir experience. We usually sing in 4 parts - soprano, alto, tenor and bass.

We do not have auditions.

Rehearsals take place on Mondays 19.30-21.30 in St John the Divine church, High Path, Merton SW19 2JY. (near South Wimbledon tube and on the 93-bus route.)

We have a range of performers, from highly accomplished to the strictly amateur.

Our choir is supportive and gives you an opportunity to practise singing with others. Singing is a great antidote to the stresses of everyday life and is a fun activity.We hold four concerts a year and offer our singers and audience an exciting musical journey with choral music and songs from the Renaissance to modern day musicals

The choir provides us with sheet music and scores and we have access to a website where we can hear recordings of individual parts, which we can sing along to. The music can be challenging but is very rewarding. The choir was founded in 1946 and is a registered charity number; 1165091

Annual membership is £170.00, payable annually or termly. Concessions are available. Associate members are welcome.
